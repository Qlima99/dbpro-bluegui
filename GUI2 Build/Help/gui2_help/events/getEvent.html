getEvent
getEvent

Description:
Retrieves information about events which have happened to gadgets in your application. When the user clicks a gadget, moves the mouse over it, or interacts with it in some other way, Windows sends information to the gadget about the event. Use getEvent once at the top of your program's main loop, then use eventSource(), eventType(), eventData() and eventDataEx() to access the information.
